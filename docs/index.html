Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

1. Data Collection

BackTrack ("the Extension") facilitates navigation within AI chat interfaces. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

2. Permissions

The Extension requests permission to access specific domains (e.g., chatgpt.com, claude.ai) solely to:

  • Scan the DOM (Document Object Model) to identify your prompts.
  • Inject the floating navigation panel.

No content from your chats is ever sent to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension performs all processing locally on your device. It does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.
